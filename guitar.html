The Guitar!

A guitar is a 6 string instrument with a fretted fingerboard that can be played by either plucking or strumming the strings. There are many different kinds of guitars, but the two most popular types are acoustic guitars that are typically much cheaper and can incorporate percussion and strumming and electric guitars that use amplifiers to strengthen and manipulate the sound.

Online/Self Taught

Ultimate Guitar Tabs

Ultimate Guitar Tabs is a great tool that is best for any experience level from beginner to expert. They have a vast archive of songs that include both professional and user created adaptations, chords, and tablatures. It is best used to learn your favorite song and new chords.

Chordify

Chordify is a website dedictated to learning guitar songs. You can play chords along with the song and choose from a vast library of songs.

Tonesavvy

Tonesavvy is a website dedictated to teaching you all of the basics of music theory. This is a great resource for anyone who doesn't have any instrumental experience and wants to start at square one.

In Person

Community Music Center

Community Music Center makes high quality music accessible to people of all ages, backgrounds and abilities, regardless of financial means. Classes are offered on a sliding scale and some are completely tuition-free.

Blue Bear School of Music

Blue Bear is a non-profit music school that strives to bring music programs to underrepresented communities. They offer classes on a variety of genres that include pop, rock, jazz, blues, folk, and more.
