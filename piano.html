The Piano!!

A piano is a stringed keyboard instrument where the strings are struck by wooden hammers that are coated with a softer material. A piano is made of 88 keys and the player (YOU) presses the keys to strike the strings with hammers. There are many types of pianos, from a small electric keyboard to a grand piano. Some genres of piano are baroque, classical, renaissance, jazz, and 21st century.
